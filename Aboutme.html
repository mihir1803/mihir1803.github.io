About me:

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Mihir Patel, a passionate Data Scientist based in Pittsburgh, PA, currently pursuing my Master of Science in Data Analytics at Robert Morris University.

My journey began at LJ College of Computer Applications, Gujarat, where I earned my Bachelor of computer applications. During my undergraduate studies,I participated in SDLC ( Software Development Life Cycle).

With one year of hands-on experience from internships at Shriram Fortune and Infolabz, I’ve developed a versatile skill set. At Infolabz, I focused on Website using Python or Django, specializing in API integration and enhancing user experiences. And, at Shriram Fortune, I expanded into data analytics, mastering Python and key libraries like NumPy, Pandas. I’ve also built insightful Power BI dashboards and developed machine learning models for applications such as bone fracture detection and vegetable condition assessment.

I’m driven by a passion for innovation and problem-solving, blending my expertise in mobile development and data science to approach challenges from fresh perspectives. I continuously seek opportunities to contribute to impactful projects and collaborate with others. Outside of work, I enjoy listening to music, hiking, and connecting with new people.
